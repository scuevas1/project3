Huevos con Chorizo

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 8 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 7 oz of Chorizo
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • Sour cream
  • Oaxaca cheese
  • Queso fresco
  • Corn tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Cook the chorizo in pan over medium heat
  2. While the chorizo is cooking, beat the eggs and milk in the bowl
  3. Once chorizo is cooked add the eggs and milk mixture and put it on medium to low heat
  4. While that is cooking, put the tortillas in the microwave for 30 seconds to soften
  5. Once the eggs and chorizo are done cooking build the breakfast tacos. Add the eggs, chorizo, sour cream, oaxaca cheese and queso fresco onto the tortillas

Arepas con Queso

Prep Time: 7 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 22 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pre-cooked arepa flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup of queso fresco
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tsp of salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine arepa flour, warm water, salt, butter, and mozzarella cheese
  2. Once combined, knead the dough with your hands for about 4 minutes while moistening your hands with water
  3. Form 5 small balls with the dough, then flatten each ball with your hands
  4. Add butter to a pan over medium to low heat. Place arepas in pan and cook for about 3 and a half minutes on each side or until each side is fully cooked and crispy
  5. Once cooked, place the arepas on a plate and top each arepa with queso fresco and mozzarella cheese

Banana Bread

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 60 minutes

Total Time: 70 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and butter a 8x4 inch loaf pan
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Then stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, sugar, baking soda, and salt. Then fold in the flour and walnuts.
  4. Once mixed, pour the batter into the buttered loaf pan and bake for about 60 minutes or until fully baked.
  5. Once fully baked, remove from oven, let it cool and serve.

Lava Cake

Prep Time: 12 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Total Time: 24 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ice cream, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F. Butter and lightly flour 6 custard cups and place them on a baking sheet.
  2. Microwave the butter and bittersweet chocolate in a large bowl on high until the butter is melted, so about 1 minute. Whisk until the chocolate is also melted.
  3. Then stir in the sugar until nicely mixed. Whisk in the eggs and egg yolks and the vanilla extract. Stir in the flour and evenly divide the mixture among the custard cups.
  4. Bake until the sides are firm and the centers are soft, about 12 minutes.
  5. Once baked, remove from oven, let it cool for about 1 minute and serve with ice cream.
